# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 8, 2024**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MY.Radio Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data We Access

When you use the Bot, we may access:
- **Server ID**: To identify which server the bot is operating in
- **Voice Channel ID**: To join and stream audio to voice channels
- **User ID**: To process command interactions

### 2.2 Data We Store

**We do NOT permanently store any user data.** The bot operates in memory only and does not maintain databases or logs of:
- User messages
- Command history
- Voice activity
- Personal information

## 3. How We Use Information

Information accessed during bot operation is used solely to:
- Process slash commands
- Join voice channels for radio streaming
- Provide bot functionality

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Transfer your data to external services
- Log or monitor your conversations

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Radio Streams
The bot streams content from third-party radio broadcasters. These streams are:
- Publicly available internet radio streams
- Not affiliated with or controlled by us
- Subject to their own terms and policies

### 5.2 Discord
The bot operates on Discord's platform and is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

## 6. Data Security

- No user data is stored permanently
- All data processing occurs in volatile memory
- The bot does not have access to private messages or channels it's not invited to

## 7. Children's Privacy

This bot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The bot is designed for general audiences and complies with Discord's age requirements.

## 8. Your Rights

You can:
- Remove the bot from your server at any time
- Request information about data we access (none is stored)
- Contact us with privacy concerns

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## 10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact the bot developer through the GitHub repository.

*By using MY.Radio Bot, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.*
